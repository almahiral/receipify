## Project Idea

Receive e-receipt (by scanning/taking pictures) from grocery stores and the AI generates recipes based on the ingredients we bought and have at home. 

For example: we buy a potato and AI will generate the recipes what we may like made by potatoes.

## Tech Stack
- Front-end: Flutter
- Back-end: Django
